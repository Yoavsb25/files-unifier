Getting Started with PDF Batch Merger
=====================================


What's in this folder
---------------------
PDF Batch Merger.app   — The application
license.json           — Your personal license (keep it next to the app)
Getting_Started.*      — This guide (txt, pdf, html)

Step 1 — Download & extract
---------------------------
1. Open the Google Drive link you received.
2. Click the file name, then click the download icon (↓) in the top-right corner.
3. Once downloaded, double-click the ZIP file to extract it. A new folder will appear — open it to find your files.

Step 2 — Place your files
-------------------------
1. Drag "PDF Batch Merger.app" to your Applications folder.
2. Copy "license.json" to the same Applications folder, next to the app.

Step 3 — Open the app
---------------------
1. Open your Applications folder.
2. Right-click "PDF Batch Merger.app" and choose Open.

  ⚠  You may see a message saying "Apple cannot verify the developer." This is normal for software not sold through the App Store. Right-click the app → click Open → click Open again in the dialog.


Step 4 — First launch
---------------------
A green checkmark at the top means your license is active. You are ready to go.
Use the three Browse buttons to select:
1. Your input file (CSV or Excel with the list of files to merge)
2. The folder containing your PDF files
3. The folder where merged files should be saved
Click "Run Merge" to start.

Need help?
