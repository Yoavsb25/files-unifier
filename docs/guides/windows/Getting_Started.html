Getting Started with PDF Batch Merger

What's in this folder

PDF Batch Merger.exe — The application

license.json — Your personal license (keep it next to the app)

Getting_Started.* — This guide (txt, pdf, html)

Step 1 — Download & extract

  1. Open the Google Drive link you received.
  2. Click the file name, then click the download icon (↓) in the top-right corner.
  3. Once downloaded, right-click the ZIP file and choose "Extract All", then click Extract. A new folder will appear — open it to find your files.

Step 2 — Place your files

  1. "PDF Batch Merger.exe" to any folder you like (for example, your Desktop or Documents folder).
  2. Copy "license.json" to the same folder as the .exe file.

Step 3 — Open the app

  1. Double-click "PDF Batch Merger.exe" to launch.
⚠ "Windows protected your PC" may appear. This is normal for new software. Click "More info", then click "Run anyway".

Step 4 — First launch

A green checkmark at the top means your license is active. You are ready to go.

Use the three Browse buttons to select:

  1. Your input file (CSV or Excel with the list of files to merge)
  2. The folder containing your PDF files
  3. The folder where merged files should be saved

Click "Run Merge" to start.
